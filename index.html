Gellian Hotel

Gelian Hotel is a luxurious accommodation located in the heart of Machakos, offering a comfortable and convenient stay for both business and leisure travelers. The hotel features elegantly designed guest rooms and suites, all equipped with modern amenities and stylish furnishings that exude a welcoming ambiance. From the moment you step into the lobby, you'll be greeted with warm and friendly staff who are committed to making your stay unforgettable. Gelian Hotel offers a range of on-site dining options, including a restaurant serving delicious international cuisine and a cozy lounge where you can unwind with a drink. With its prime location in Machakos, exceptional service, and top-notch facilities, Gelian Hotel is the perfect choice for your next stay in the city

Machakos University Hotel

Machakos University Hotel is a premier accommodation located right beside Machakos University and the origin of Biqq, a revolutionary app that allows guests to order food from the comfort of their own rooms. The hotel boasts a range of beautifully appointed rooms and suites, each offering breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape. With its prime location, exceptional service, and top-notch facilities, Machakos University Hotel is the perfect choice for visitors to the area. The hotel also features an on-site restaurant serving a variety of mouth-watering dishes, as well as a cozy lounge where you can unwind with a drink. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, Machakos University Hotel promises an unforgettable experience.
